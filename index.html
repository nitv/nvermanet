Nitin Verma, Ph.D.

I am a Postdoctoral Research Scholar in the AI & Society program jointly organized by the School for the Future of Innovation in Society (SFIS) at Arizona State University (ASU) and the New York Academy of Sciences (NYAS). My broad research interest is in investigating the notion of trust in information and information technologies, and in studying how society and advanced AI could co-shape each other. My doctoral dissertation (at the School of Information at The University of Texas at Austin) was focused on how AI-based synthetic audiovisual media (such as deepfake videos) influence public trust in video and in visual journalism.
